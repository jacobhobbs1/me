2025 Journal Article AR

AR Interactions in Radiology: Design Considerations Towards an AR-First World

Virtual Worlds, MDPI — 2025

A qualitative study exploring how practising radiologists and radiology registrars envision augmented reality in their clinical environment. Seven interviews were analysed using reflexive thematic analysis and NASA-TLX, yielding five themes and three design considerations for AR-first radiology workflows.
